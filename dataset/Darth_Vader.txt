Darth Vader, once Anakin Skywalker, was a Jedi Knight who fell to the dark side. He became Sith Lord under Emperor Palpatine. He killed the Jedi younglings, fought Obi-Wan on Mustafar, and wore the black suit after being burned. Father of Luke Skywalker and Leia Organa. He kills the Emperor to save his son and redeems himself, dying as Anakin again.
