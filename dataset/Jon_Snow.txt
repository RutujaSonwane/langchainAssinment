Jon Snow is the bastard son of Ned Stark, raised in Winterfell. He joins the Night's Watch to find purpose. Later revealed to be Aegon Targaryen, son of Rhaegar and Lyanna. He becomes Lord Commander, falls in love with Ygritte, kills Daenerys to stop her madness, and is exiled beyond the Wall with the Free Folk. His siblings are Arya, Sansa, Bran, and Rickon Stark. He is known for honor and leadership against the White Walkers.
