Harry Potter is a young wizard who survived Voldemort's killing curse as a baby, earning the title "The Boy Who Lived". He attends Hogwarts, discovers he is a Parseltongue, plays Seeker in Quidditch, and forms Dumbledore's Army. His best friends are Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. His parents were James and Lily Potter, killed by Voldemort. He finally defeats the Dark Lord in the Battle of Hogwarts.
